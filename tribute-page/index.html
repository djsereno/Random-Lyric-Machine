Neil Peart

"From the point of ignition. To the final drive. The point of the journey is not to arrive." - Neil Peart

Here are some of Neil Peart's most memorable lyrics and quotes:

  • "The more we think we know about, the greater the unknown" - Mystic Rhythms, Power Windows (1985)
  • "You can twist perceptions, reality won't budge" - Show Don't Tell, Presto (1989)
  • "Sometimes our big splashes are just ripples in the pool" - Emotion Detector, Power Windows (1985)
  • "We each pay a fabulous price for our visions of paradise" - Mission, Hold Your Fire (1987)
  • "Floating high in the evening sky, I see my faint reflection; pale facsimile, like what others see, when they look in my direction" - Earthshine, Vapor Trails (2002)
  • "All four winds together can't bring the world to me; shadows hide the play of light so much I want to see" - Available Light, Presto (1989)
  • "You can do a lot in a lifetime if you don't burn out too fast" - Marathon, Power Windows (1985)
  • "Some will sell their dreams for small desires or lose the race to rats, get caught in ticking traps, and start to dream of somewhere to relax their restless flight" - Subdivisions, Signals (1982)
  • "When I leave I don't know what I'm hoping to find, and when I leave I don't know what I'm leaving behind" - The Analog Kid, Signals (1982)
  • "The measure of a life is a measure of love and respect; so hard to earn, so easily burned" - The Garden, Clockwork Angels (2012)
  • "If you choose not to decide, you still have made a choice" - Freewill, Permanent Waves (1980)
  • "I can't pretend a stranger is a long-awaited friend" - Limelight, Moving Pictures (1981)
